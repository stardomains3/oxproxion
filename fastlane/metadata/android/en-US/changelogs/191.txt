🗺️ Native Google Maps Navigation
You can now ask the AI to start turn-by-turn navigation directly. This tool integrates with the Google Maps app to provide a hands-free transition from chatting to driving.
•	Turn-by-Turn Navigation: Launch Google Maps instantly by providing a destination name, address, or coordinates.
•	Custom Travel Modes: Support for Driving, Walking, Bicycling, and Public Transit.
•	Route Preferences: Optionally tell the AI to avoid tolls, highways, or ferries.
•	Intelligent Defaults: Defaults to driving mode unless specified otherwise, and automatically handles address encoding for better accuracy.
🛠️ Technical Improvements
•	Enhanced Tool Support: Added the start_navigation tool to the internal toolset.
Example commands to try:
•	"Navigate to the nearest Starbucks avoiding tolls."
•	"Start walking directions to the Empire State Building."
•	"Drive to 1004 N Spruce St, Ogallala, Nebraska."
